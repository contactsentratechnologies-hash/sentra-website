Most growth ceilings aren't strategic. They're operational.

The companies we work with rarely have a strategy problem. They have an operations problem — tools that don't connect, customer messages that slip away, knowledge trapped in a few people's heads, and numbers that arrive too late to matter. We exist to fix exactly that.

01

Your business runs on disconnected tools.

Sales in one system, inventory in another, accounting in a third. Every report is a fresh argument about which numbers are correct.

02

Customer messages slip through the cracks.

WhatsApp, email, web chat, calls — nothing in one place. Response times are slow. Some customers never come back.

03

Institutional knowledge walks out the door.

The answer to every question lives in one busy person's head. New hires take six months to become useful.

04

You can't see what's actually happening.

Reports arrive late. Numbers disagree. By the time leadership spots a problem, it has already cost the business.

The platform

The Sentra Suite. Four products. One platform.

Each product is engineered to resolve a specific operational pain. Used together, they form an integrated platform that runs your entire business — under your brand, in your cloud, owned by you.

01

Atlas

Business Operating System

For when: sales, inventory, accounting, HR, projects and fleet are scattered across half a dozen tools that don't agree with each other.

A single, unified platform that holds your entire operation in one place. Your team logs in once and sees the whole business. Reports come from the same live data. The data-shuffling work simply disappears.

  • End-to-end ERP — sales, finance, inventory, HR, projects, fleet
  • Live, single source of truth across every team
  • Built around your products, prices and terminology
  • Integrates with your bank, suppliers, channels and existing tools
02

Echo

Customer Support Platform

For when: customers reach you across seven channels, your team checks five inboxes, and conversations regularly go unanswered.

A single command centre for every customer conversation — WhatsApp, email, web chat, social, voice. AI drafts replies in your tone, your team approves and sends in one click. Nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Omnichannel inbox — every channel, one screen, full history
  • AI assistant drafts replies in your voice, escalates the rest
  • Auto-routing, SLA monitoring, escalation alerts
  • Connected to your customer record in Atlas
03

Cortex

Private AI Brain

For when: your company's knowledge lives in scattered documents, inboxes and people's heads — and onboarding a new hire takes months.

A private AI assistant trained on your documents, your processes, and your way of working. Anyone in your team can ask anything and get the right answer in seconds, with the source. Your knowledge stops walking out the door.

  • Private LLM — your data never leaves your environment
  • Answers with citations to the source document
  • Connects to your documents, drives, wikis and chat history
  • Available in chat, on WhatsApp, and inside every Sentra product
04

Lens

Executive Analytics

For when: the numbers leadership needs arrive days late, in different formats, and rarely agree with each other.

A live executive dashboard built around the metrics that matter to your business, monitored continuously. When something looks off, you receive a clear explanation on your phone — before the issue compounds.

  • Real-time dashboards across sales, ops, finance, customer health
  • Automated anomaly detection with plain-English explanations
  • Daily executive briefing delivered to your phone
  • Embedded directly inside Atlas for in-context decisions

Security & trust

Engineered to enterprise security and compliance standards.

The platforms we deliver protect customer data, financial records and operational continuity. Security is not a layer we apply at the end — it is part of the architecture.

Data residency & ownership

Deployments run in your AWS account by default. Your data, your encryption keys, your control. Available regions across USA, EU and UK to satisfy data-residency requirements.

Encryption end to end

AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. Secrets stored in AWS Secrets Manager. No service is exposed to the public internet other than the application gateway.

Private AI by design

Your Cortex deployment runs on dedicated infrastructure inside your environment. Your documents and customer data are never sent to external model providers.
