How We Build It

From Signed To Live In Weeks. Not Years.

We don't disappear into a six-month black box. Every Friday, you see real progress. Every milestone, you decide whether to move forward. There are no surprises, no scope creep, no junior consultants reading from a script — just one senior team building your platform alongside you.

1. Discovery & Design

Week 1

A senior engineer sits with you and your team. We learn how your business actually works — the language, the workflow, the quirks. You leave the week with a clear written plan and a fixed price.

2. Built Around Your Business

Weeks 2–3

Your platform takes shape with your branding, your terminology, your workflows. You see live progress on Friday demos — never a surprise at the end. We adjust as you watch, not after.

3. AI & Integrations

Weeks 3–5

Your AI assistants learn your data, your tone, your processes. We connect everything that needs to talk to your existing tools, your bank, your suppliers, your channels. Your team starts test-driving real workflows.

4. Launch & Aftercare

Week 5–6

We move you to live, with hand-holding training, a written runbook, and a senior engineer on call. The platform is yours — full IP, full ownership, no lock-in. We stay on as your support team for as long as you want us.

✓

Our risk-reversal: If the first milestone doesn't meet the specification we agreed in writing, you don't pay for it. That's how confident we are in this delivery model — and how much we respect your time and money.
